Project Z


Project Z is a Sci-fi dungeon crawler game. My role here is technical game designer in a team of 15 people but led the level design side.

• Collaborated closely with the concept artist to design a 10-room dungeon that included puzzles, traps, and enemy encounters.
• Utilized Unreal Engine to block out 9 rooms and refined them through multiple gameplay iterations.
• Generated one-page documents for each room to share with the team, streamlining the production process.
• Developed AI spawners to test the flow and difficulty of each room, ensuring optimal gameplay experience.
• Programmed a Diablo-style camera feature that dynamically hid objects in the level, resulting in improved gameplay for the game.


Tools & resources used:
• Unreal Engine 5
• Photoshop
• Google Documents

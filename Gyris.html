Gyris

Gyris is a game where I worked as UE5 Developer in a team of 15 people. Some of the tasks I made:

• Collaborated with the level designer by implementing blocking volumes and set-dressing the world to support the overall design vision.
• Devised, created, and implemented a spawn system that randomized obstacles on the track, resulting in dynamic and varied gameplay for each race.
• Utilized blueprints to implement destruction features across multiple assets.
• Designed and developed a background level for a character selection menu, from initial concept to functional prototype.

Tools & resources used:
• Unreal Engine 5
• Maya
• Substance 3D Painter
• Photoshop

Windows build available on itch.io.

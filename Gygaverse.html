Gygaverse


Gygaverse is a company made of 10 people. My role here was level designer creating spaces for people using VR headset. The purpose of these levels is to have concerts with famous singers using a mocap and selling tickets to assist online.

• Conceptualized, Blocked out, set dressed and optimized VR levels for the Oculus platform.
• Made a script to teleport the players from one building to another to have a more dynamic experience.


Tools & resources used:
• Unreal Engine 5
• Maya
• Blender
• Photoshop
• Google Docs
